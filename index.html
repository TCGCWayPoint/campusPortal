About Our Project

The Tangub City Global College QR Code-Based Navigation Campus Map Portal aims to enhance the campus experience by providing an intuitive and user-friendly navigation system. Our goal is to make exploring TCGC effortless and enjoyable for everyone.

Key Features

  • Interactive Campus Map: Easily locate buildings, facilities, and points of interest.
  • QR Code Navigation: Scan QR codes around the campus for instant location details.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for simplicity and ease of use.
  • Feedback System: Share your thoughts and help us improve the portal.
